Our Heritage

Gainesville's Family Cobblers

Keeping North Georgia Walking Since 1988

Gainesville Shoe Repair isn't just a shop—it's a staple of our community. Established in 1988 by the Embry family, we have spent over 35 years refining the craft of hand-crafted leather repair and sole reconstruction.

Led by head cobbler Doug Embry, we believe in preservation over replacement. In a world full of disposable fashion, we stand for durability. We use heritage tools combined with modern adhesives to guarantee your boots return tougher than they left.

"If you buy a quality pair of leather shoes, they shouldn't just last you a couple of seasons. With proper care and timely resoling, they should last you a lifetime."

— Doug Embry, Founder
